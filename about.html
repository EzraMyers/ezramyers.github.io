Ezra Myers is a contemporary artist working across drawing and mixed media.

His practice is rooted in raw mark-making, using ink, charcoal, and collage to construct layered figurative works.

Through hyper-controlled realism and embedded symbolism, the work explores identity, mythology, and inherited memory.

Each piece functions as both portrait and structure, revealing narratives shaped by distortion, presence, and cultural memory.
